To provide our core AI features, we utilize the Google Gemini API.

Subscriptions are linked to your Apple ID. Your payment information is subject to Apple's privacy policy. You can restore your purchases on any device using the same Apple ID via the "Restore Purchases" button in the app.

6. Data Deletion (Account & Profile)

Although no traditional account is created, Vokey Vo provides a "Delete Account" (or "Clear All Data") feature within the Application settings.

  • Effect of Deletion: Upon selecting "Delete Account," all locally stored data, user preferences, and the anonymous identifier associated with your device will be permanently removed from our active records.
  • Once deleted, this data cannot be recovered, and any Pro entitlements may need to be restored via your Apple ID.
